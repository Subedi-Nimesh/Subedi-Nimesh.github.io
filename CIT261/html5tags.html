HTML5 tags

Video:

The video tag can be used to play clips in the webpage.
It has different attribute to make it look better.

Example:
Code of Video:

<'video width="320" height="240" controls>
<'source src="funnyVideo.mp4" type="video/mp4">
Your browser does not support the video tag.
<'/video>

Audio:

The Audio tag can be used to play audio in the webpage.
It has different attribute to make the controls look better with functions.

Example:
Code of Audio:

<'audio controls>
<'source src="Memories.mp3" type="audio/mp3">
Your browser does not support the audio element.
<'/audio>
